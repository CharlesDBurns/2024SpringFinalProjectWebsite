Cloudland Canyon State Park

Description:

Cloudland Canyon is one of the largest and most scenic parks in the state of Georgia located on the western edge of Lookout Mountain. The gorgeous scenery of this park is home to deep canyons that plunge as deep as a thousand feet, towering sandstone cliffs, breath-taking waterfalls, enticing wild caves along with dense woodland that bring a since of adventure into the camping experience. The park offers ample outdoor recreation with its abundant wildlfe and delightful scenery as stated before. Along with hiking and mountain biking, the park also offers a multitude of other activites if interested, like fishing, disc golf, trails for horseback riding, sites for picnicking and overlooks. Visitors seeking an overnight experience can choose from a fully-equipped and comfortable cottage, a quirky yurt, or several different types of camping and backpacking options.
SelfBurns Productions recommends this park for it's gorgeous views and variety of activities availiable.
