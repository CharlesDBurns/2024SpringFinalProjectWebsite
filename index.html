About Us

Welcome to the camping section of our website! A brief summary of the parent website: SelfBurns Productions is a company that creates multiple forms of media like animation, literature and games, it also offers sites like this offering information. It was founded in 2024 with the goal of helping others in the creation of said forms of media and more, helping people create what they want and share it with the world. To this end of helping people, sites like this were created to help give information on various topics. The information on these sites that is offered varies, each themed and assembled for those who are not as knowledgeable on certain topics. This website is dedicated to the outdoor activity of camping, giving information on the type of gear needed, desired locations and of course others' experiences to help get people interested in making similar stories of their own. For that has been the goal of SelfBurns Productions to begin with: to help people make stories of their own.

Camping

Camping is the act of spending time outside in nature to admire it or to take part in particularly challenging activities like rock climbing, canoeing or hiking. Typically this experience is done over the period of one day or more where you would sleep outside as well, usually in a tent but some sleep in cabins or RVs when they camp. When choosing to camp there are a variety of things that you need to think about before hand, like what kind of gear will you need to take with you? Where should you go to have your camping experience? Along with these, you'll probably have more questions and hopefully this website will help you at least answer some, as well as inspire you. The home page contains just this introductory information as well as a gallery of camping images to get you thinking about camping. The Gear page will help you in learning what you may need to think about when looking for gear as well as what gear you may need along with a checklist that may help you track what you need and have. The Experiences page contains small experiences shared about camping experiences, showing what kind of stories can be formed when participating in this outdoor activity. Finally, the Locations page contains a location that we recommend to those who are new to camping along with a map of this location. Hopefully this website will be of help to our visitors!
